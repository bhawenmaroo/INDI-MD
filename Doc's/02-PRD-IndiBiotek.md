## 1. Project Summary

IndiBiotek Private Limited is a biotechnology company founded in 2024 and registered in August 2025, headquartered in Kolkata, West Bengal, with offices in Bengaluru, Delhi-NCR, Guwahati, and Andaman & Nicobar. The company operates across three divisions — Lifesciences, Agri, and Scientific — and is pioneering the indigenous development and manufacture of biotech products that India currently imports from countries including China and Taiwan.

This project is a complete redesign and rebuild of the existing website at indibiotek.com. The new website replaces the existing GoDaddy-hosted site at the same domain. The goal is to create a world-class, visually premium website that establishes IndiBiotek's credibility with investors and grant bodies, educates buyers across three professional audiences, and drives email inquiries through a clean, functional contact system.

Success means: a visitor from any of the five target audience groups lands on the site, immediately understands what IndiBiotek does, trusts that it is a legitimate and serious company, and knows exactly how to contact the team.

---

## 2. Goals

1. **Build immediate credibility** — Within the first scroll, every visitor sees IndiBiotek's patent, government certifications, and R&D partnerships, and feels confident this is a serious company.
2. **Educate multiple audiences clearly** — Farmers, doctors, lab owners, purchase officers, and VCs should all find relevant information quickly without reading everything.
3. **Drive email contact inquiries** — At least 15% of visitors should click Contact or submit the form.
4. **Create a strong first impression** — The visual design should be the best-looking biotech website a visitor has seen from an Indian company.
5. **Represent the Make in India mission** — Every page reinforces IndiBiotek's core differentiator: building India's own biotech manufacturing capability.

## 7. How It Should Look and Feel

**Vibe:** Premium, sober, and clean. Confident and authoritative. Inspired by terminal-industries.com — dark, bold, cinematic without being gimmicky.

**Typography:** Poppins (Google Fonts) — headings, body, captions, navigation.

**Colour palette:**
- Main: Deep Navy `#0A1628` — navigation, headings, dark mode backgrounds
- Accent: BioGreen `#00C896` — buttons, hover states, highlights
- Supporting: Soft Steel `#4A90A4` — secondary text, decorative elements
- Light mode bg: `#FFFFFF` with subtle `#F0F7FF` surfaces
- Dark mode bg: `#080F1E` with `#0D1830` surfaces

**Background per page:**
- Home hero: DNA / molecular helix 3D (Three.js / Vanta.js)
- Division pages: Slow animated gradient in each division's colour — Lifesciences (blue), Agri (green), Scientific (teal)
- Contact, Services, About: Static — nothing distracts from content or form

**Dark mode:** Available from launch via toggle. Both modes fully designed.

## 8. Performance and Quality Standards

- Load in under 3 seconds on Indian 4G mobile
- Works perfectly on small smartphone screens
- Contact form reliable — every submission reaches info@indibiotek.com

- Works in Chrome, Safari, and Firefox — desktop and mobile
- Light/dark toggle switches instantly with no flicker
- 3D animation pauses on slow/older devices automatically
