## IndiBiotek Private Limited
### Company Profile — v1.0 | May 2026

---

### Who We Are

IndiBiotek Private Limited is an Indian biotechnology company building indigenous solutions for healthcare, agriculture, and science. Founded in 2024 and formally incorporated in August 2025, we are headquartered in Kolkata, West Bengal, with a national presence in Bengaluru, Delhi-NCR, Guwahati, and the Andaman & Nicobar Islands.

We are a BIRAC-supported startup, recognised under the Government of India's Startup India programme, registered with the MSME Ministry, and proud to carry the Make in India certification. Our patent application (No. 202511077832) is on file with the Indian Patent Office.

---

### What We Do

IndiBiotek operates across three integrated divisions:

**Lifesciences:** We develop AI/ML-powered portable diagnostics (the ASSURED device combining LAMP, microfluidics, and CRISPR/Cas), molecular testing kits, ELISA, CLIA, rapid test formats, and point-of-care devices for human health, food safety, dairy, veterinary, poultry, and aquaculture applications. We also develop indigenous reagents, buffers, and chemicals for research and clinical use.

**Agri:** We manufacture and supply a complete range of biological agricultural inputs — biofertilizers, bioinsecticides, biopesticides, biofungicides, bioherbicides, plant growth regulators, and bio-organics — designed to improve Indian crop yields while reducing chemical dependency.

**Scientific:** We supply high-quality reagents, buffers, chemicals, lab consumables, and instruments to academic institutions, research labs, hospitals, and industrial facilities across India.

---

### Our Story

India currently imports the majority of its diagnostic kits, agri-biotech inputs, and scientific reagents from countries including China and Taiwan. This dependency costs India in foreign exchange, supply chain resilience, and lost opportunities for Indian scientists and entrepreneurs.

IndiBiotek was founded to change this. We believe India has the scientific talent, the institutional infrastructure, and the national resolve to build world-class biotech products at home. Every product we develop is a step toward making Indian healthcare more resilient, Indian farming more sustainable, and Indian science more self-sufficient.

---

### Our Research Foundation

**SRM University, Delhi-NCR** — Centre for Drug Design Discovery & Development (C4D), Sonipat, Haryana. A dedicated facility for drug discovery, diagnostics development, and biotech innovation.

**Adamas University, Kolkata** — E-YUVA Centre, supported by BIRAC (Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council), a Government of India body under the Department of Biotechnology. IndiBiotek is one of a select group of startups chosen for BIRAC's E-YUVA programme, which provides infrastructure, mentorship, and funding access to early-stage biotech companies.
